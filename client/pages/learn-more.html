About HealthCare+

Your comprehensive healthcare management solution

🎯 Our Mission

At HealthCare+, we strive to make healthcare management simple, efficient, and accessible for everyone. Our platform connects patients with healthcare providers seamlessly.

💡 How It Works

For Patients:

  1. Register: Create your account with basic information
  2. Choose Department: Select the medical department you need
  3. Book Appointment: Pick from available dates and time slots
  4. Get Confirmation: Receive your appointment details with room number
  5. Visit Hospital: Show up at your scheduled time

For Doctors:

  • View daily appointment schedules
  • Access patient information
  • Manage appointments efficiently
  • Track patient visits
